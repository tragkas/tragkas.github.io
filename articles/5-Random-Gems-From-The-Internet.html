5 Random Gems From The Internet

by George Tragkas

Here’s the list:

1. Get advice only from people who have done the things they advice you to do.

2. Have you ever wonder if you could have a coffee with one of your role models? Because this is not very likely, the next best thing is try to find online interviews or talks from the person you admire.

3. Did you know what the most successful people in the world have in common? - The same 24 hours that you have. Do you know what makes them different from us? - The way they use these hours.

4. Things and situations have the value you assign to them, if you don't give them, they don't have it.

5. Start, then learn. Don't learn, then start. Whatever you want to build, a program, a business or a relationship it requires 100 steps. Don't waste your time in steps between 2-100 just focus on the first step. Start then everything else will follow.

Enjoy.
