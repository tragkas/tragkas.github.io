The default man

by George Tragkas

I believe there are two kinds of people, classic right? But seriously there are two kinds of people; I identify them as the default dead and the default alive. What does it even mean to be a default dead person? We can picture him like this: a person wakes up in the morning and goes to school or work, trying to find something that will fill the void inside him. The next day repeats the process only to find out that "the boss" is wrong "the pay" is low, the schooling is bad, or it might be the weather, right? It rained yesterday. Superficial problems that are not meant to be solved.

On the other hand, we have the default alive person, the story goes like this: a person wakes up in the morning and goes to school or work, trying to find something that will fill the void inside him. The next day repeats the process but with a twist. This person knows and accepts the void, doesn't try to blame others or the circumstances for his own weaknesses, knows "the boss" is not wrong "the pay" is enough and the schooling is good because all of them pre existed, he just chose to participate.

This is the big difference between the two: the default dead doesn't know any better and follows the crowd, while the default alive can always choose to leave. At the end of the day, it's just a choice.

Are you dead or alive?


How to recognize them:

The default dead:

  • Works for something that doesn't love
  • Wakes up with someone who doesn't love
  • Lives for the paycheck
  • Lives for the retirement
  • Lives for the day that will never come

The default alive:

  • Is happy, leave him alone.

Enjoy.
