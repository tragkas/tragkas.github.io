10 Things You Should Do As an Undergraduate Student

by George Tragkas

Here’s the list:

1. Get to know the area. Familiarize yourself with your new surroundings. You can search on YouTube walks near the area or using
Google maps with the street view tool. The best one though is to visit in person the area and explore.

2. Visit a Campus party. Alone or with colleagues the experience is worth to try.

3. Use your student pass at its fullest. You will be surprised how beneficial can be, just search about it.

4. Travel somewhere as a student. Plan ahead and use your student pass if able.

5. Learn to cook. Ask any student to list the top three concerns about living in the Campus and you will see the “food problem” in all of them. A good advice is to learn how to make your favorite recipes or to find a cheap place near your campus area.

6. Create a weekly budget and keep track your expenses. It is a valuable skill that it will follow you for a lifetime.

7. Get involved with many activities. This will make you explore new things, create new memories and acquire new skills.

8. Find the people you admire and explore their story, their career, the paths they chose to follow.

9. Adopt a pet (especially a cat). I know it doesn’t sound ideal but it will keep you company through the difficult semesters and will make you
more responsible knowing you have to care for a living thing.

10. Throw a party. Remember that we are nothing before the universe, we are just plankters (fancy word right?) in a small pond. So create a moment that you will never forget.

Enjoy.
