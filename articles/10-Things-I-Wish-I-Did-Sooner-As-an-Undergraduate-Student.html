10 Things I Wish I Did Sooner As an Undergraduate Student

by George Tragkas

Here’s the list:

1. Create valuable friendships, afterwards these friends will be your network for life (network = net worth).

2. Make you professors remember you by name.

3. Get constantly advised by your professors, their advice is free but it is worth a lifetime of experience.

4. Help your teachers if you are able to, they will help you back (probably).

5. Be ahead of your game, just out of curiosity visit other classes to see if the things you believe you want are really worth pursuing (it will save you some time).

6. Visit forums, Facebook groups or discord servers, you will be amazed what you can find online about your University or your major (trust me).

7. That’s an easy one: Whatever problem, essay or thesis you facing Just search it on Google or YouTube.

8. Read books about your Major. I know what are you saying, are you crazy? I have already so much to read. That’s ok but sometimes, new things about a topic can help you discover ideas that you never explore in your studies.

9. Start something alongside your degree, whatever it is: a new language, another diploma or a sport (after 4 years it will worth the sacrifice).

10. Create memories for you and your friends.

Enjoy.
