10 Bits of Wisdom From My Professors to You

by Georgios Tragkas

Here’s the bits:

1. Your most valuable asset, is your name.

2. If you don’t plan your life, someone else will design it for you.

3. Repetition is the mother of learning.

4. Dedication is the right hand of success.

5. Enjoy the moments you have while you have them.

6. What intensifies fear and desire is ignorance.

7. Nothing is worth if you don’t keep your word.

8. Act with good manners even if others are not.

9. Say NO if you don’t want something.

10. Every relationship loses some of the thrill over time. That’s inevitable.

Enjoy.
