How I use YouTube to learn and not waste time

by Georgios Tragkas

YouTube is a beautiful place for us to learn, see and find new things but at the same time is a huge waste of time if we don't use it to build our life.

First, I find a good channel that I can learn from and then I make a playlist with the most useful content of that channel.

Second, I watch all the content inside the playlist while keeping notes.

Third, I move on to the next channel.

Enjoy.
