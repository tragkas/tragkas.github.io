How I use YouTube to learn and not waste time

by George Tragkas

YouTube is a captivating realm offering a wealth of knowledge and discovery. However, it can also become an extensive source of distraction if not channeled towards personal advancement.

Here's a systematic approach I follow to harness the potential of YouTube for personal growth:

  1. Identification: Begin by pinpointing reputable channels that align with your learning objectives and interests.
  2. Compilation: Create a purposeful playlist, meticulously selecting the most pertinent content from these channels.
  3. Engagement: Dive into the playlist content with intention, actively taking notes to capture key insights.
  4. Expansion: Gradually diversify your exploration by delving into content from different channels, broadening your horizons.

By adhering to this methodical approach, YouTube's potential transforms from a potential time drain into an invaluable instrument for personal development.

Enjoy.
